TwitterPPDB: The Large Scale Paraphrase Database in Twitter


The Corpus

  • The TwitterPPDB is a collection of sentence level paraphrases from Twitter by linking tweets through shared URLs. This corpus is the largest up to date with 51,524 human annotated sentence pairs: 42200 for training and 9324 for testing. It can grow 30,000 new sentential paraphrases per month with ∼70% precision. Now we have 1-year data available: 2,869,657 candidate pairs!

    The following paper introduces the corpus in detail:
    A Continuously Growing Dataset of Sentential Paraphrases
    Wuwei Lan, Siyu Qiu, Hua He and Wei Xu. EMNLP 2017.
